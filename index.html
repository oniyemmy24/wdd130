Osun

Osun is a state in western Nigeria with population of over 4,000,000. The state capital is Osogbo.It share border with other four states in western region of Nigeria. Osun's economy is based mainly on agriculture. Major crops include yams, cassava (manioc), corn (maize), beans, millet, plantains, cacao, palm oil and kernels, and fruits. Cottage industries produce brass work, woven cloth, and wood carvings.
